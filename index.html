Roedel Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Projevity Pty Ltd (ACN 696 573 154), the operator of Roedel, collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Account Data

When you create an account, we collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Username
  • Password (hashed — never stored in plain text)

2. Profile Data

You may optionally provide: age, gender, height, weight, body fat percentage, fitness goals, activities, and region. All profile data is controlled by you and can be updated or removed at any time.

3. Health & Fitness Data

When you enable third-party integrations (such as Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin, Whoop, Fitbit, Strava, Hevy, or Samsung Health), the app may import the following numeric health metrics only:

  • Active calories
  • Resting calories
  • Daily calorie expenditure
  • Sleep duration and/or score
  • Activity levels
  • Workouts (calorie totals only)

Roedel does NOT import: Heart rate or medical records, GPS routes or location data, Workout details, or Social posts from external apps.

6. AI Data Processing (Coach Mando)

Coach Mando (an AI-powered coach) may analyse your nutrition logs and macro adherence to provide personalised coaching insights. AI-generated insights are informational only and do not constitute medical advice.

12. Your Rights & Data Controls

You have the right to access, download, correct, or delete your data. Account deletion will permanently remove your profile data, meal posts, and health data imports within 30 days.

14. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, data is removed within 30 days unless required by law.
