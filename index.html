Roedel Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Projevity Pty Ltd (ACN 696 573 154), the operator of Roedel, collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Account Data

When you create an account, we collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Username
  • Password (hashed — never stored in plain text)

2. Profile Data

You may optionally provide: age, gender, height, weight, body fat percentage, fitness goals, activities, and region. All profile data is controlled by you and can be updated or removed at any time.

3. Health & Fitness Data

When you enable third-party integrations (such as Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin, Whoop, Fitbit, Strava, Hevy, or Samsung Health), the app may import the following numeric health metrics only:

  • Active calories, Resting calories, and Daily calorie expenditure
  • Sleep duration and/or score
  • Activity levels and Workouts (calorie totals only)

Roedel does NOT import: Heart rate or medical records, GPS routes or location data, or social posts from external apps. Only minimal data needed for calorie expenditure calculations and coaching insights is imported.

4. Usage Data

We collect data related to your app usage, including: meal logs, posts, comments, reactions, group memberships, and challenge participation.

5. Device Data

Device type, operating system, and app version for performance optimization.

6. AI Data Processing

Coach Mando (an AI-powered coach) may analyse nutrition logs (calories, protein, carbs, fat), macro adherence, calorie expenditure, sleep data, and activity levels. This analysis is used solely for personalised coaching within the app. AI-generated insights are informational only and do not constitute medical advice.

7. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to: provide and improve the service; calculate nutritional estimates and goals; power Coach Mando for personalised guidance; display leaderboards and social features; and detect abuse or fraud.

8. Data Sharing

Projevity Pty Ltd does not sell your personal health data. Your data may be shared with: other users (based on your settings), infrastructure providers, analytics services, and law enforcement if required by law.

9. Advertising & Tracking

Roedel may display advertisements. You can control whether ads are personalised through the "Personalised Ads" setting. On first launch, you will be asked for consent to analytics tracking and ad personalisation.

10. Third-Party Services

Roedel integrates with Open Food Facts for product data via barcode scans. Health integrations use secure OAuth authentication. Projevity Pty Ltd is not liable for the data accuracy or privacy policies of third-party integrations.

11. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and HTTPS. Row-Level Security (RLS) is employed to protect personal biometrics, private posts, and sensitive profile data.

12. Your Rights & Data Controls

You have the right to access, download, correct, or delete your data. Account deletion will permanently remove your profile data and health imports within 30 days.

Roedel strives for global data excellence. Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have rights under the GDPR, and North American users have rights under applicable consumer protection laws (including CCPA), including the right to data portability and the 'Right to be Forgotten'.

13. Privacy Controls

Roedel provides "Ghost Mode" to make your profile invisible to non-friends and granular controls for post visibility. You may disconnect any third-party integration at any time.

14. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

15. Children's Privacy

Roedel is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically and will notify users of significant changes through the app.
